Our facility

Dog House offers both AC and non-AC kennels of different sizes to suit dogs of different breeds. While the more heat-intolerant breeds require AC in the hot hours of the day, we also host many Indian hounds and other large breeds who prefer our airy and spacious non-AC kennels that allow them a view of our lush-green outdoors.

Dog House offers FREE and UNLIMITED swimming to dogs boarding with us. Swimming, besides helping your dog beat the sweltering Chennai heat, is also great exercise and can be therapeutic for Arthritic dogs.

Dog House's swanky ride for the VIPs ( Very Important Pets) is equipped to escort dogs all over Chennai. If your dog is boarding with us, we offer pickup and drop regardless of which corner of Chennai you're in!

Dog House is a 15,000-sft. ( 6 grounds) property flaunting a massive, open-to-sky play area. Dogs love to run and play in our huge yard. We offer a beautiful, lush-green environment, fully secured by high walls.
