Dog Breeds

1. Labrador

Labrador Retrievers (commonly known as Labradors) are sweet-faced, loveable and adorable! One of the most popular dog breeds in India, Labradors are outgoing, friendly, and have enough love in them for the whole family! The Labrador, a great socialiser, rarely has a bone to pick with neighbouring dogs.

-Life expectancy 10-14 years

2. Golden Retriever

Originally from Scotland, Golden Retrievers are in demand as they are caring, intelligent and very obedient. They make good watchdogs, are an active dog breed in India, and can be trained for dog shows and competitions. Golden Retriever s puppies are sure to make your heart melt! Keep them active and entertained with these dog toys.

-Life expectancy 10-12 years

3. Rottweilers

Rottweilers are powerful, dominant and yet very loyal and friendly dogs. They have a short coat and tend to shed fur frequently. Lovely as family pets, and are super intelligent during training sessions.

4. The Shih Tzus

One of the small dog breeds in India, Shih Tzus have been companion dogs to even Chinese royalty! Their name means ‘little lion’ in Mandarin, and have always been considered warm, gentle yet agile and playful furry ones who love to cuddle! Explore dog food best suited for small breeds.

-Life expectancy 10-16 years

5. The Pomeranian

Pomeranians are one the small dog breeds in India. They are affectionate and friendly, and it is easy to teach them tricks. A Pomeranian can be your best friend if you can keep up with their energetic, curious selves!

-Life expectancy 10-16 years

6. The cocker spaniel

Cocker Spaniels were initially bred as hunting dogs, and are a crossbreed of the American Cocker Spaniel and the English Cocker Spaniel. They have a silky coat and floppy ears. Their medium-size, adorable looks and smart minds make them easy to train, great playmates and the best dog for home in India! Check out these dog treats, perfect for training
